Who am I? Who are you?! How did you find this site, we are still working on it!

The Dreamlands are a beautiful magical place. A shared place where those with a strong enough will can create anything their heart desires. Everyones subconious thoughts all blend into an amalgamation of odd symbolisms that our waking selfs could only, well, dream of. It creates a fantastic play room for Keepers and Players to truly embrace their inner pure whimsical self, explore your humanity with truly no limits of logic.
